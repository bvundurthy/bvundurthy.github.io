Research Interests

My current research interests are control systems and decision sciences (game theory and optimization) with applications in the analysis of engineering and economic systems.
In particular: theory and computational aspects of dynamic games, hybrid optimal control, impulse optimal control, networked multi-agent systems.
I am also interested in exploring applications of game theory in smart grids, supply chains, etc.
